HTML+CSS & Marks+Channels

Data Types: The pie charts are positioned on an x-y plane, so their data type is position. The pie charts themselves show categorical attributes in their color so their data type is attribute (specifically categorical). The fraction of the pie being taken up by each category is a quantitative data type. But also the size of the pie chart shows a quantitative attribute data type.
Marks: The marks of this visualization are both points and Areas/Volumes. The pie charts are used as points on the x-y plane but within each pie chart, area is used to convey the data. These are items and not links
Channels: The channels being used here are both horizontal and vertical postion, color, area size, and possibly tilt.
